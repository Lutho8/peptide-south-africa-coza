name: Consultation booking
description: 1:1 Zoom consultation booking is a mailto-only flow to webinars@fintiba.com — no payment, no PayPal.
type: feature
---
Consultation bookings are handled via a `mailto:webinars@fintiba.com` link with subject "1:1 Peptide Consultation Request" and a prefilled body. There is no payment step, no PayPal, and no price displayed. The CTA lives in `BookCallSection.tsx` on the home screen.
